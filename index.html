About Me

Hi there, I'm Theodore. I'm a second year student at the University of Manchester studying Computer Science. I have a keen interest in Software Engineering, Systems Engineering and Game Development. I'm also fascinated by trains, submarines and ships - how they work and what they can be used for.

In my spare time I like to make games, short films and sometimes tinker with hardware or robots. You can find some examples of the things I've done here. I'm always up for working on something new, feel free to contact me if you have any ideas or need help on a project!

Contact me here if you want to collaborate on a project or for recruitment.
